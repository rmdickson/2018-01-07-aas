Who: The course is aimed at astronomers at all stages of their education and careers who wish to learn computational tools to increase the reproducibility and efficiency of their work. The course will be taught with the assumption that participants have written or edited code in a language other than Python and can navigate directories using the shell command line. Knowledge of Git is not required.

Registration is for both days. Participants will be required to bring their own laptops and to install software in advance of the workshop.

We encourage participants to apply their newly developed skills at the Hack Together Day.
